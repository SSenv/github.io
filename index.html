Frequently Asked Questions

What types of e-waste do you accept?

We accept all types of electronic waste including computers, laptops, servers, mobile phones, tablets, printers, copiers, televisions, refrigerators, air conditioners, washing machines, and other electronic appliances.

How can I schedule an e-waste pickup?

You can schedule a pickup by calling us, sending us an email. We'll arrange a convenient time for collection.

Is my data safe when I dispose of my electronic devices?

Absolutely! We understand the importance of data security. Our certified data destruction services ensure complete wiping or physical destruction of storage media following international standards. We provide a Certificate of Data Destruction upon completion.

Do you provide certificates for recycled e-waste?

Yes, we provide official Certificates of Recycling/Disposal for all e-waste processed through our facility. These certificates include details such as the type and quantity of waste, date of collection, and recycling method used, which can be used for compliance and sustainability reporting.

What happens to the e-waste after collection?

After collection, we transport the e-waste to our CPCB-authorized facility where it undergoes a systematic process: registration and weighing, sorting by category, testing for potential reuse, dismantling, segregation of components, resource recovery, and responsible disposal of non-recyclable materials. Throughout this process, we maintain strict environmental and safety standards.

Are there any charges for e-waste collection?

Our pricing varies based on the type and quantity of e-waste, as well as your location. For certain high-value items, we may offer buyback options. Please contact our team for a customized quote. For large corporate clients, we offer special contract rates.

How can my company benefit from your buyback program?

Our buyback program offers fair market value for functional/non-functional electronic equipment. This provides your company with financial returns on unused assets, reduces storage costs, ensures proper disposal, and contributes to your sustainability goals. All equipment undergoes secure data wiping before evaluation.

What are your certifications and authorizations?

SS Envirocare is fully authorized by the Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) and State Pollution Control Board (SPCB) for e-waste recycling.

Do you conduct awareness programs on e-waste management?

Yes, we offer customized awareness programs for corporations, educational institutions, and communities on responsible e-waste management. These programs include presentations, workshops, and educational materials designed to promote sustainable practices and regulatory compliance.
